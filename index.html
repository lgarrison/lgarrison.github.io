About Me

I am a Flatiron Research Fellow at the Flatiron Institue's Center for Computational Astrophysics. Previously, I was a graduate student at the Harvard-Smithsonian Center for Astrophysics working with Daniel Eisenstein. My current research is on high-performance cosmological $N$-body simulations and the formation and statistics of large scale structure. My general research interests include cosmology and high-performance computing.

I am the lead developer of the Abacus cosmological N-body code, which I work on with Daniel Eisenstein and Nina Maksimova. I also develop the Corrfunc code with Manodeep Sinha.
